About

Hi, I'm Emily

I was born at the start of a new millennium in "Smalltown," Wisconsin. I currently study Communication Arts with a Film, Radio, and Television focus at the University of Wisconsin-Madison.


From the beginning of my memories, I've always loved fiddling around with technology. Although I'm only starting to learn the technical side of it, my curiosity knows no bounds to what I'm willing to discover. I hope to someday work as an Art Director in the video game industry.
